Setup

What are you setting up?

  1. Get an XLR and a monitor box from the In-Ear Monitors box.
  2. Get a rechargeable battery from one of the chargers.
  3. Plug the battery into the box, and make sure the box is set to MONO (not STEREO).
  4. Connect your box to the correct output at the back of the stage with an XLR (see below).
  5. Connect your headphones or monitors to the box.
  6. Turn the box ON.
  7. Connect to the "Tenth Gym" network with password: earsacce$$
  8. Use the Qu-You app to set your mix and control who you hear.

  1. Get a mic stand, an XLR, and a vocal microphone.
  2. Set up your mic where comfortable.
  3. Plug your mic into channel 15, 19, or 21.

If you ARE using MainStage:

  1. Get a power bar, an XLR, and a stage bug.
  2. Set up the keyboard where comfortable.
  3. Place your laptop on a stand beside the keyboard.
  4. Use a MIDI cable (not provided) to connect your computer and the keyboard.
  5. Plug the 3.5 mm Stage Bug cable into your laptop's headphone jack.
  6. Using the XLR, connect the Stage Bug to channel 16.

If you ARE NOT using MainStage:

  1. Get a power bar, an XLR, a DI, and a quarter-inch.
  2. Set up the keyboard where comfortable.
  3. Connect the DI and the keyboard (LEFT OUT) by the quarter-inch cable.
  4. Using the XLR, connect the DI to channel 16.
